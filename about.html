Hello! Thanks for dropping by.
I'm a QA analyst, web designer, photographer and musician from beautiful Vancouver, Canada.

I currently work in the tech industry as a QA analyst focusing on helping startup founders build great products. I enjoy testing software applications, It's a very challenging and technical job that allows me to think outside of the box and exercise the logical side of my brain. I'm a visionary who keeps in balance with logical and creative thinking.

I believe it's important to maintain a healthy work-life balance. When I'm not in the office, you can catch me hiking & camping, attending photography meet-ups or working on DIY projects. I see each day as an opportunity to learn something new, and strive to learn something new each and everyday.
