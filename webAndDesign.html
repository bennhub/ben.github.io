Samurai Sushi

Known for their larger than normal portions at an affordable price, Samurai Sushi is a popular local favorite spot for sushi lovers and my goal is to re-design their website to help improve the user experience and ultimately increase conversions.

Upon evaluating the original Samurai Sushi website, I observed some issues that would negatively impact the user experience. The primary areas that could be improved include the site’s layout design and visual hierarchy. The layout design, for example, was greatly affected by the lack of alignment of the website’s visual elements. Also with a high percentage of customers ordering take-out, the website needed to be responsive for friendly viewing on mobile devices.

My main goal is to keep it simple, a one page minimalistic layout design that's easy on the eyes, allowing the user to easily navigate to each section with the push of a button.

Brewmapper

Brew Mapper's objective is to help craft beer lovers easily locate local craft breweries and pubs that sell craft beer in Vancouver. The goal is to implement a simple responsive design that uses google maps as a it's main feature to pin point brewery locations.

A contiunous project in the works, I donate my time to Brew Mapper when needed. This is a fun project, enabling me to practice UX/UI design, coding, social networking, photography and most of all, drink great beer and get to know the craft brewery business owners for possible future projects.
