My Music & Audio Apps

My introduction to music occurred at the age of 10 when I first picked up my father's guitar, igniting a lifelong passion. Since then, music has remained an integral part of my life, consistently prioritized and cultivated. I pursued formal education in music recording and sound design at Columbia Academy. Through this program, I acquired comprehensive skills in recording, editing, and mixing music, alongside a profound understanding of audio post-production for various media formats, including TV, film, and game audio. Additionally, I have a keen interest in coding audio applications and players using the Web Audio API, allowing me to combine my passion for music with my technical skills in web development.
