About

Beatloops is a scratch loopers project created by Ben Hayes. It aims to provide a platform for DJs and musicians to explore and experiment with loops in a creative and intuitive way. The project combines the power of the Scratch programming language with a library of high-quality audio loops.

With Beatloops, users can create dynamic and unique musical compositions by combining and manipulating loops in real-time. The intuitive interface allows users to easily layer loops, adjust their tempo, pitch, and apply various effects to create their own distinctive sound.

The project provides a range of features and tools to enhance the creative process. Users can access a vast library of loops across different genres and styles, ensuring there is something for everyone. Additionally, Beatloops offers advanced features such as loop slicing, time-stretching, and MIDI control, opening up endless possibilities for musical exploration and performance.

Whether you're a seasoned DJ or a beginner musician, Beatloops offers a seamless and intuitive platform to unleash your creativity and take your music production to the next level. Join the community of artists and musicians who are pushing the boundaries of loop-based music with Beatloops.

Key Features

  • Intuitive loop-based music creation
  • Real-time loop manipulation and layering
  • Extensive library of high-quality audio loops
  • Loop slicing and time-stretching capabilities
  • Tempo and pitch adjustment
  • Advanced effects and sound processing
  • MIDI control for enhanced performance
  • Collaborative features for remote music production
  • Seamless integration with other music production tools
