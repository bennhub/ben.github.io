About Brewmapper

Vancouver, BC. A craft beer mecca for beer drinkers. With all these craft beer places popping up, you may find it a bit overwhelming trying to decide on which one to choose. So why not make it easier by visiting the closest craft brewery to your current location. Brewmapper exists to help you explore the local craft beer scene by locating them on google maps.


I created this project for pure enjoyment. This site is fully responsive, you can view it on mobile devices. Still in BETA form, I will continue to make updates as much as I can. I'm also open to suggestions, feel free to contact me with your ideas on how to improve Brewmapper. If any breweries want their spot on the map, shoot me a mail and I'll add it. Contact: brewmapcontact@gmail.com

Cheers to the beers!
