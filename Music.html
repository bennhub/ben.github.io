My first experience with music was at the age of 10, when I first picked up my Dad's guitar. Since then music has always been apart of my life, it's something that I've always made time for.

I studied music recording and sound design at Columbia Academy. From this I've learned how to record, edit, and mix music; I also developed a deep understanding in audio post-production for TV, film and game audio.
